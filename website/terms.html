2. Description of Service

AutoCaptioner is an Android application that allows you to add AI-generated captions and subtitles to your videos using on-device speech recognition. Its core features include:

  • On-Device Transcription: Speech recognition is performed entirely on your device using integrated Whisper AI models. Your video and audio content is never uploaded to any server for processing.
  • Caption Generation & Editing: The App generates caption files and allows you to customise their appearance and timing.
  • AI Model Downloads: To enable transcription, the App downloads AI language models from our distribution servers upon first use or when a new language is selected. An internet connection is required for this step.
  • In-App Purchases (AutoCaptioner Pro): We offer digital in-app purchases to unlock premium features. These purchases are securely processed through Google Play and managed via RevenueCat.
