Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 29, 2026

Welcome to AutoCaptioner! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). We are currently in closed testing, and we value your privacy and trust.

1. 100% On-Device Processing

AutoCaptioner is built with a privacy-first approach. All video and audio processing, including speech recognition and caption generation, happens locally on your device using our integrated AI (Whisper) models.

  • No Cloud Uploads: Your media files (videos, audio, photos) are never uploaded, sent, or stored on our servers.
  • No Audio Streaming: Audio data is analyzed purely on your device's hardware.

2. Data Collection and Use

Because the App operates entirely on-device for its core features, we collect very minimal data. Below is a full and honest account of what is collected and why:

  • AI Model Downloads: The App requires an internet connection to download the necessary AI models (e.g., Whisper language models) upon first use or when selecting a new language. This request goes to our model distribution servers. No media or content is sent during this process.
  • Crash Reports (Firebase Crashlytics): We use Firebase Crashlytics to receive automatic crash reports. When the App crashes, Crashlytics collects diagnostic data including your device model, Android OS version, app version, a stack trace of the error, and the state of the app at the time of the crash. Your media files, video content, captions, and personal data are never included in crash reports.
  • Usage Analytics (Firebase Analytics): We use Firebase Analytics to understand how the App is used in aggregate. This includes anonymised events such as which screens are visited and which features are used, along with device information and approximate location at the country or city level. No personal media content is collected or transmitted.
  • Advertising ID (AD_ID): The App declares the Advertising ID permission, which is used solely by Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics for analytics correlation and crash reporting purposes. The App does not display advertisements and does not use the Advertising ID for ad targeting or profiling of any kind.
  • Performance Monitoring (Firebase Performance): We use Firebase Performance Monitoring to measure technical performance metrics such as app startup time, screen render times, and network request durations. This data is anonymised and aggregated.
  • Remote Configuration (Firebase Remote Config): We use Firebase Remote Config to manage feature flags and app configuration remotely. This service fetches configuration parameters from Firebase servers. No personal data is sent as part of this process.
  • Paid Features: In the future, we plan to introduce paid features. When these are launched, we will update this policy regarding payment processors. No financial data is processed at this time.

3. Device Permissions

To function correctly, AutoCaptioner requests the following permissions:

  • Storage / Media Permissions: Required to select videos from your gallery and to save the final captioned videos back to your device.
  • Internet: Required solely to download the language AI models and future updates.

4. Third-Party Services

AutoCaptioner uses the following third-party services. We do not sell or rent your personal information to anyone.

  • Google LLC / Firebase: We use several Firebase services (Crashlytics, Analytics, Performance Monitoring, and Remote Config) operated by Google LLC. These services may collect device identifiers, app usage events, crash diagnostics, and performance metrics as described in Section 2. All data collected by Firebase is processed by Google under their own privacy policy. You can review Google's Privacy Policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Your Media Stays Yours: None of the above third-party services receive your video files, audio recordings, or generated captions. Your media content never leaves your device.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

AutoCaptioner processes all media entirely on your device and does not upload your media or captions to our servers. We do not store or have access to your personal media or generated captions, so you do not need to request media deletion from us.

Crash reports, anonymised analytics events, and performance metrics collected via Firebase are retained by Google according to their data retention policies. If you have questions about how Google retains this data, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy. If you wish to delete locally saved output files, you can do so by removing them from your device's storage or by uninstalling the App.

6. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take necessary actions.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
